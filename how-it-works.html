How It Works

From your data to the consumer's hand.

Two products. Two data layers. One QR code.

BluePrint

Your story. Your depth. One QR code.

BluePrint is how consumers see your work. Start with your farm story. Add product-level data when you're ready. Everything lives behind a single QR code on the pack.

Farm Level

Your farm story

Set once. Applies to every product in your range.

  • Farm name and location
  • Farm video
  • Photo gallery
  • Founder or farm manager quote
  • Origin map
  • Certifications
  • Species and farming method

Product Level

Your product data

Set per SKU. Add as many or as few as you like.

  • CO₂e per kg
  • FIFO ratio
  • Feed certification and sourcing
  • Production journey
  • Processing location
  • Harvest date
  • Stocking density
  • Welfare practices

One farm profile form. One SKU form per product. Blue. structures, verifies, and writes everything.

BlueScore

A verified rating on the pack.

BlueScore takes your BluePrint data and turns it into a single A-F rating. Like Nutri-Score, but for how fish was farmed. Printed directly on packaging.

Verified

Calculated directly from your BluePrint data. Every input is structured and checked.

Simple

One letter grade. A-F. Consumers understand it instantly at the shelf.

Independent

Open methodology. The same data always produces the same score.
