How It Works

From your data to the consumer's hand.

Three products. Two data layers. One QR code.

The Products

Three products. Each one builds on the last.

Tier 1

BluePrint Basic

The farm story. One setup covers your whole range.

  • Farm video and photo gallery
  • Founder or farm manager quote
  • Origin map and location
  • Certifications and species
  • QR code for every product in your range

Unlocks: Starter tier

Tier 2

BluePrint Full

The product story. Applied to your chosen SKUs.

  • CO₂e per kg
  • FIFO ratio
  • Feed certification and sourcing
  • Production journey and processing location
  • Harvest date and stocking density

Unlocks: Growth tier

Tier 3

BlueScore

The verified rating. A–F on packaging, calculated from verified data.

  • A–F rating displayed on packaging
  • Calculated from BluePrint Full data
  • Like Nutri-Score for how fish was farmed
  • Independent verification methodology

Unlocks: Scale tier

The Data

Two data layers. Set once, apply everywhere.

Producer Level — Set Once

Your farm profile

  • Farm name and location
  • Farm video
  • Photo gallery
  • Founder quote
  • Origin map
  • Certifications
  • Species
  • Farming method

Product Level — Set Per SKU

Your product data

  • CO₂e per kg
  • FIFO ratio
  • Feed certification
  • Production journey
  • Processing location
  • Harvest date
  • Stocking density
  • Welfare practices

One farm profile form. One SKU form per product. Blue structures, verifies, and writes everything.

The Experience

What the consumer sees.

BluePrint Basic

The farm story: video, photos, location map, certifications, and a quote from the producer.

BluePrint Full

Everything in Basic plus product-level data: CO₂e, FIFO ratio, feed sourcing, production journey.

Full + BlueScore

Everything in Full plus a verified A–F rating. One clear number that summarises how the fish was farmed.
