About

Blue. exists because seafood has nothing to hide.

A consumer-facing transparency platform for aquaculture. Verified production data, visible at the shelf.

Blue. takes verified production data from fish farms and turns it into something a shopper can actually see. On the pack, at the shelf, at the moment they decide what to buy.

The Problem

The problem is not the data

Good producers already collect detailed data about how their fish is farmed. Emissions figures, feed conversion ratios, stocking densities, certifications. It all exists. The problem is that none of it reaches the consumer.

A pack of farmed salmon in a supermarket looks the same whether it comes from a farm with industry-leading practices or one that barely meets the minimum standard. The data is there. The communication layer is missing.

The Gap

Existing tools solve a different problem

Most traceability platforms in seafood are built for B2B compliance. They help producers report to retailers, auditors, and certification bodies. That is important work. But it does not help a consumer standing in front of a fish counter.

Blue. is not a traceability system. It is a communication system. It takes data that has already been collected and verified, and presents it in a format that consumers can understand and trust.

The Approach

How Blue. is different

Blue. gives producers two products: BluePrint (your farm and product story) and BlueScore (an independently verified A-F rating). A QR code on the pack connects the consumer to a structured, verified profile.

No PDFs. No downloads. No industry jargon. Just clear, specific information about where their food came from and how it was produced.
