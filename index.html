How It Works

Two products. One clear stack.

BluePrint makes your data visible. BlueScore turns it into a rating.

BluePrint

Your farm and product story in a structured, verified profile. Video, photos, origin map, certifications, environmental data, and production journey. Accessed via a QR code on every pack.

BlueScore

An independently verified A-F rating printed directly on packaging. Calculated from your BluePrint data. Score breakdown by category, with the data behind every grade.

Every producer starts with BluePrint. BlueScore adds a verified rating when you're ready.
